Our Story

Lumina: Crafting quality essentials for intentional living since 2018.

Born from a Desire for Simplicity

Lumina was founded on the belief that true luxury lies in simplicity. We reject fast trends and fleeting styles, focusing instead on creating timeless pieces designed to last a lifetime.

Our founders, Jane and Mark, were frustrated by crowded closets and disposable goods. They launched Lumina in a small studio with the mission to curate a collection where every item serves a purpose, offering superior quality without complexity.
